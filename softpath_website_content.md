## Marketing Infrastructure Audit

Before you scale spend or buy another tool, find out if your current setup is telling you the truth.

In 2-3 weeks, I'll audit your tracking, martech stack, and paid accounts, then give you a prioritized roadmap to fix what's broken.

**What's included:**

**Tracking & Attribution Assessment**
- Pixel and tag implementation review (Meta, Google, TikTok, GA4, GTM)
- Event taxonomy and conversion tracking validation
- Attribution gap analysis
- Cross-platform data consistency check

**Martech Stack Assessment**
- Tool inventory: what you have, what you're paying, what's actually used
- Data flow mapping: how data moves between systems (or doesn't)
- Configuration review for core platforms (Segment, Klaviyo, HubSpot, ActiveCampaign)
- Recommendations: keep, fix, consolidate, or kill

**Paid Acquisition Audit**
- Account structure evaluation across active platforms
- Spend efficiency analysis (90-day or 6-month window depending on sales cycle)
- Creative and audience organization review
- Measurement validation: do platform numbers match reality?

**What you get:**
- Written report with findings and supporting data
- Slide deck for stakeholders
- Live walkthrough call to review findings and discuss next steps
- Prioritized 90-day action plan
